Notice of Funding Opportunity (NOFO; previously "Funding Opportunity Annoncement") summaries. AI (primarily OpenAI's GPTs) was used to summarize funding opportunities pdfs via a retrieval augmented generation pipeline, powered by Langchain and Langgraph. You can find the code that was used to do all of this at github.com/matthewcarbone/Composer.

Caution: these summaries are AI-generated

Use at your own risk, and always check the funding opportunity itself. Do not rely on these summaries when submitting a response!
