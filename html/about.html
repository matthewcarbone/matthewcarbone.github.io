About me

I am an experimental chemist by training, but became a theoretical condensed matter physicist in graduate school. Over time, I have also begun to incorporate many more data-driven methods into my work, including machine learning. Currently, I study a variety of topics, including condensed matter, computational spectroscopy and how we can use data-driven techniques to accelerate discovery and aid interpretation/analysis in science.
